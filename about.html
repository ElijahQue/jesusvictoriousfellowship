What We Believe

Our foundational biblical convictions

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our final authority in faith and life.

Jesus Christ

We believe in Jesus Christ as the Son of God, our Savior, who died and rose again for our salvation.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the Holy Spirit who empowers believers for life and service in God's kingdom.

The Church

We believe the Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and ministry.

Salvation

We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone, not by works.

Second Coming

We believe in the personal, visible return of Jesus Christ to establish His eternal kingdom.
